Weather & Time-Based Mutations

Wet (x1.2)
Harvest any crop while it is raining.
Drenched (x5)
Harvest any crop during a thunderstorm.
Chilled (x2)
Harvest any crop during the Winter season in-game.
Moonlit (x2)
Harvest any crop during the night.

Item & Ability-Based Mutations

Gilded (x1.5)
Have a chance to get this mutation when harvesting a crop affected by the Midas Touch star ability.
Radioactive (x80)
Grow and harvest crops within the Nuclear Wasteland area.
Zombified (x5)
Let the Undead Stalker (Zombie) from the Graveyard biome walk over your crops.
Cooked (x10)
Use the "Heat" star ability on your crops.
Burnt (x4)
A less valuable version of Cooked, also from the "Heat" star ability.

Special & Event Mutations

Gold (x20)
A rare, more valuable version of Gilded from the Midas Touch star ability.
Rainbow (x50)
Obtained from the Leprechaun during the St. Patrick's event or other special events.
Voidtouched (x135)
A rare mutation obtained from crops affected by the Void-touched Obelisk.
Celestial (x120)
Obtained from crops planted on the Celestial Patch in the Zen Garden.
